How Easy Is It to Install Shipsi on Shopify?

Ridiculously easy. Here's how to go live with Shipsi in less than 5 minutes:

Step-by-Step: Installing the Shipsi Extension in Shopify

  1. Log in to your Shopify Admin Panel
    Go to your Shopify dashboard at shopify.com/admin.
  2. Visit the Shopify App Store
    Navigate to the Shipsi Shopify App Store page and search for "Shipsi".
  3. Click "Add App"
    On the Shipsi app page, click the "Add app" button to initiate installation.
  4. Grant Permissions
    You'll be prompted to grant permissions so Shipsi can sync with your inventory and orders. Approve the request.
  5. Configure Your Preferences (Optional)
    Set your preferred delivery regions, time windows, and pricing.
  6. Go Live!
    That's it. Shipsi is active, and your customers can now start selecting instant delivery at checkout.
